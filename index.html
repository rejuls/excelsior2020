What Makes Excelsior2020 Different?

Excelsior 2020 is the second iteration of the flagship event of IEEE SB GEC Palakkad. Attended by about 200 dynamic techno enthusiasts, the event marks a technological tinkerer’s opportune moment to be a part of integration of information, inspiration and collaboration. Catering to inquisitive minds, it is a culmination of several interactive keynotes by some of the best minds in technology as well as workshops based on the peer learning model. This technical festivity seeks to provide latitude for networking with young bright minds and illicit techno-enthusiasm by transporting them to a world of technophilia, technovation and techtainment.
